Why Maker/Taker Fees Matter for Your Portfolio

# Exchange

Maker: You place a limit order that waits to be filled. You 'make' liquidity — exchanges reward this with lower fees.
Spot Taker ?
Taker: You place a market order that executes immediately. You 'take' liquidity — exchanges charge more for this.
